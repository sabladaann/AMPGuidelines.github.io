PASOA ATTENDANCE MONITORING PROGRAM

"PASOA ATTENDANCE MONITORING PROGRAM is an automated recording of attendance utilizing the Near-Field Communication technology. The NFC attendance monitoring will help each student and school staff to track attendance easily and in a short period of time."

HOW TO USE:

  • Gently tap your NFC card on the scanner
  • Wait for the scanner to display a red light to confirm your NFC card was scanned
  • Keep your appearance decent while you stand in front of the camera
  • Before you leave for your class or event, make sure that a checkmark shows on the screen to verify that your phot was taken correctly
  • Remember to repeat these steps to sign out after your class or event

REMINDERS:

  • Take care of your card to avoid damage and being lost. Preferably place it in your ID holder for safekeeping.
  • Don't forget to bring your card to school.
  • Before taking your photo, make sure your face is well-lit to ensure a clear photo
  • Only one person should sign in at a time to avoid errors or system delays.
  • Please remove hats, sunglasses, or any accessories that may obstruct your face before taking your photo.
