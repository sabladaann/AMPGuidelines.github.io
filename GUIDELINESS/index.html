PASOA ATTENDANCE MONITORING PROGRAM

"PASOA ATTENDANCE MONITORING PROGRAM is an automated recording of attendance utilizing the NFC technology. The NFC attendance monitoring will help each student and school staff to track attendance easily and in a short period of time."

HOW TO USE:

  • Gently tap your NFC card on the scanner
  • Wait for the scanner to display a red light to confirm your NFC card was scanned
  • Position yourself in front of the camera and makes sure to look presentable
  • Ensure you see a check mark on the screen to confirm your photo was captured before entering class and event
  • Remember to repeat these steps to sign out after your class

REMINDERS:

  • Take care of your card to avoid damage and being lost. Preferably place it in your ID holder for safekeeping.
  • Bring it every time you are attending school.
  • Before taking your photo, make sure your face is well-lit to ensure a clear photo
  • Please ensure only one person signs in at a time to avoid errors or delays
  • Remove any hats, sunglasses, or accessories that may obstruct your face
